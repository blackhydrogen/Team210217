How to use Let's Fund

As a Patron:

  1. Browse through a variety of projects waiting for your donations
  2. View the details about individual projects and the teams behind them
  3. Enter how much you want to donate to the business
  4. Manage the businesses that you have funded in your dashboard

Make a change in a new entrepreneur's business. If you feel that the business has not met your expectations, you can refund your donation.

As an Entrepreneur:

  1. Create a project about your business
  2. Include some details and tags to make finding your business easier
  3. Watch as your business gets funded
  4. Manage your projects in your dashboard

Bring your project from the drawing board to real life, with the backing of people who want to see your project succeed. Keep all the donations that you get.
